Welcome to SQGATE's interactive Logic Gates Sandbox. Our free online simulator lets you drag, drop, and wire AND, OR, NOT, NAND, NOR, XOR, and XNOR gates in real time to build any digital circuit imaginable.

How to Use the Logic Gates Simulator

  • Step 1: Open the Sandbox
    Click the launch button below to enter the free SQGATE workspace.
  • Step 2: Add Logic Components
    Drag logic gates and flip-flops from the left sidebar onto the canvas.
  • Step 3: Wire Everything Together
    Connect the output pins of your gates to the inputs of the next stages. Add interactive switches and LEDs.
  • Step 4: Real-time Simulation
    Watch the live simulation as inputs change, or open the integrated oscilloscope to view interactive timing diagrams.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does it support sequential logic like Flip-Flops?

Yes. The sandbox includes D, JK, SR, and T Flip-Flops, alongside clocks and 7-segment displays for advanced sequential design.

Can I export my circuit schematic?

Absolutely. You can export high-resolution PNG images of your circuits directly from the toolbar, making it perfect for lab reports and presentations.

Can I convert my logic circuit to Verilog code?

Yes! With one click, you can generate complete Verilog or VHDL structural code for your entire circuit design.
