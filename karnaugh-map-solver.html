How to use the Karnaugh Map Solver

A Karnaugh Map (or K-Map) is a visual method of simplifying Boolean algebra expressions. By organizing truth table outputs into a specific 2D grid, you can group adjacent 1s to identify redundant logic variables. This results in the simplest possible Sum of Products (SOP) or Product of Sums (POS) expression.

Using SQGATE, you can directly input your minterms or maxterms. The simulator will instantly generate the K-Map visualization for 2-variable, 3-variable, or 4-variable expressions. It will automatically group the adjacent cells and output the simplified Boolean equation, completely eliminating human calculation errors.
